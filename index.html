About Health Pravah

Ahmedabad's Dedicated
Physiotherapy &
Orthopedic Centre

At Health Pravah, we believe recovery is not just about treating pain — it's about restoring your ability to live fully. Our clinic combines evidence-based physiotherapy with personalized care plans tailored to each patient's unique condition and lifestyle.

Founded with a mission to bring world-class rehabilitation to Ahmedabad, we have helped over 2,000 patients recover from injuries, chronic pain, neurological conditions, and post-surgical complications — all under one roof.

Certified & Experienced Therapists — all our doctors hold advanced physiotherapy qualifications with years of hands-on experience.

Modern Equipment & Techniques — from electrotherapy to manual therapy, we use the latest evidence-backed treatment modalities.

Personalized Treatment Plans — no cookie-cutter protocols. Every patient gets a plan designed around their specific goals and recovery timeline.

Affordable & Accessible Care — high-quality physiotherapy should not be a luxury. We keep our treatment accessible to all.
